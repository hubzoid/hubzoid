---
name: summarize
description: Produce a 3-bullet summary of a long document.
---

When the user asks you to summarize a document, follow this playbook:

1. Identify the three most important points in the source. Skip filler.
2. Each bullet ≤ 25 words. Keep concrete numbers and proper nouns.
3. End with a single-line takeaway labelled "Bottom line:".

Do not pad. Do not editorialize.
